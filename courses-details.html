Description

College Algebra is a foundational mathematics course focusing on advanced algebraic techniques, functional analysis, and modeling real-world problems. Key topics include linear, quadratic, polynomial, rational, exponential, and logarithmic functions, along with systems of equations, inequalities, matrices, and sometimes conic sections or complex numbers, bridging high school algebra and pre-calculus
